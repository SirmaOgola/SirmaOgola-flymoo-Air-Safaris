Our Experiences

Private Charter

Experience bespoke air travel on our modern fleet. Enjoy flexibility, comfort, and exceptional service on your schedule.

Scenic Flights

Blend thrill with the calm of the skies and discover our hand‑picked, unforgettable aerial views. Maximize your private charter flight by adding a scenic flight experience – or pick a full‑day adventure where we fetch you, whisk you away, and bring you back. Whichever you choose will elevate your safari adventure, because every scenic flight gives you a fresh, bird’s‑eye glimpse of Africa from an extraordinary angle.

Adventure Safari Flights

Embark on an exhilarating aerial adventure highlighting Kenya's iconic tourist wonders. Start with a sunrise departure to gaze upon the majestic peaks of Mount Elgon, then drift over the legendary Masai Mara plains hoping to see wildlife against the stunning skyline, or swoop above the sparkling waters of Lake Naivasha. We tailor experiences to fit every dream. For northern Kenya explorations, we recommend a scenic flight to the dramatic Rift Valley and the historic Lamu Island. Each journey offers the freedom to touch down for a picnic lunch or champagne breakfast, creating unforgettable memories.

Aircraft Lease

Making the decision to purchase your own aircraft, whether for personal use, is a huge decision and should never be taken lightly. There are many variables in the decision-making process to purchase, but there is also the option to lease aircraft. Much like leasing an automobile, aircraft leasing is not for every operator but it does make more sense than purchasing for some operators. FAS provides you with consultancy services on lease management on fixed wings and helicopters.
